Proper pet care involves providing a balanced diet tailored to your pet's nutritional needs, regular exercise to maintain their physical health, and routine veterinary check-ups to monitor their well-being. Grooming your pet regularly, whether it's brushing their fur or trimming their nails, helps prevent matting, discomfort, and potential health issues. Additionally, spending quality time with your pet through play and affection fosters a strong bond and promotes their mental stimulation and emotional well-being.
